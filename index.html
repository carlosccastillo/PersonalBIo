BIOGRAPHY- Carlos C. Castillo

ABOUT CARLOS

Carlos C. Castillo was born in Lynwood, California on June 20, 1996. He graduated from El Rancho High School in Pico Rivera, California in June 2014. Carlos is a proud Hispanic American fortunate enough to be enrolled as a student. Over the past two and one-half years he has worked to become a Computer Information Systems Major at California State University, Chico. He intends to graduate with a B.A. in the Fall term of 2019.

Carlos has participated in a variety of sports, (such as soccer, baseball, track and filed, etc.) but also participates in events to support programs and/or special causes. Also, Carlos loves to volunteer to help others. With many other interests, he enjoys just about anything in life.

Thus far, Carlos has accomplished a few miniscule achievements as follows:

  1. Invented and designed the first ever computer, the ENIAC, 1946.
  2. Successfully create a time machine, 2016.
  3. Learned how to ride a bike, 2017.

In his spare time, Carlos loves to joke around and have a good time. With friends and family to support him, indulging in new adventures is what he is known for. Carlos also has a Computing Biography and Development Portfolio that further describe a few academic adventures he has participated in.

Carlos’ ongoing mission is to learn more about program writing and develop new career skills. Consequently, Carlos hopes to spread peace, love, and positivity along his mission(s). Behind all his inspiration and hopes is his idol: “You have to fight to reach your dream. You have to sacrifice and work hard for it.” [Lionel Messi, 2010]
